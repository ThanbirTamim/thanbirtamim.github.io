About Me

I am a software professional with a strong foundation in research and real-world system development, currently serving as a Senior Software Programmer in the Bangladesh Army. My work focuses on building advanced 2D and 3D GIS-based simulation systems used in military training and strategic operations. These systems blend geospatial data, real-time rendering, and procedural generation to create immersive, mission-critical environments. I am deeply passionate about education and research and aspire to pursue a long-term academic career.
